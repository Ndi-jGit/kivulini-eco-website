About Us

Discover Our Mission and Vision for a Sustainable Future

Our Mission

Kivulini Eco Park aims to blend luxury with sustainability, providing a haven that reconnects guests with nature. Our mission is to lead by example in promoting eco-conscious practices within the hospitality industry, ensuring that every guest enjoys a unique and responsible experience.

Our Vision

To become the leading eco-tourism destination in East Africa, demonstrating that luxury and environmental responsibility can coexist harmoniously while creating meaningful experiences for our guests and positive impact on our community.

Officially inaugurated by Dr. Alfred N. Mutua, the Cabinet Secretary of Wildlife and Tourism, Kivulini Ecopark is a haven for eco-conscious travelers seeking an immersive experience amidst nature's wonders. As the chief guest, Dr. Mutua, along with other distinguished attendees, marked the official launch of this awe-inspiring destination, where every corner whispers tales of conservation and harmony with the environment.
