Our Story

Launching in 2024, Kivulini Eco Park represents a new era of sustainable luxury in Nairobi. Nestled in the prestigious Karen neighborhood, our eco-conscious retreat offers an immersive experience that celebrates Kenyan heritage while championing environmental responsibility.

Our Mara-style architecture, inspired by the iconic Maasai Mara, creates an authentic safari experience right in the heart of the city, surrounded by lush greenery and modern amenities.
